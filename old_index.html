The above pathway diagrams are Sankey diagrams showing the pathways learned by the model for each particular anemia class. Each diagram is generated from the trajectories followed by the model leading to that anemia class' diagnosis. The green pathways represent the correctly diagnosed patients whereas the red pathways concluded in an incorrect diagnosis. The orange nodes depict the actions that query for feature values nodes, while the diagnosis actions are the purple nodes at the end. In addition, the size of each flow corresponds to its support, i.e., the number of patients that have it in their pathway.

Interactive Diagnostic Decision Pathways


A Brief Description

This page displays links to the interactive plots for the paper entitled "Deep Reinforcement Learning for Personalized Diagnostic Decision Pathways: A Comparative Study on Anemia and Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE) Using Electronic Health Records". The Sankey diagrams show the pathways learned by the model for the two use cases. Each diagram is generated from the trajectories followed by the model leading to that class' diagnosis. The orange nodes depict the actions that query for feature values nodes. In addition, the size of each flow corresponds to its support, i.e., the number of patients that have it in their pathway.

In the anemia Sankey diagrams, the green pathways represent the correctly diagnosed patients whereas the red pathways concluded in an incorrect diagnosis. The diagnoses are reprsented by the purple nodes at the end.

In the SLE Sankey diagrams, we show pathways from two different models which were sleceted based on their metrics, that is, accuracy and WPAHM. The three commonest pathways for each class are shown whereby each pathway is represented by a different colour. The values on the edges are displayed on hovering over the nodes. The diagnosis actions are the dark green nodes at the end.
